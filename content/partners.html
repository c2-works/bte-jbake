BTE has six major partners in the International markets.

Hegewald & Peschke Meß- und Prüftechnik GmbH provides high-quality machines, components and software solutions for material and component testing.

IPT – one of the leading providers of test systems for the plastics processing industry.

Techlab Systems (TLS) provides quality solutions for Packing and Packaging.

Tinius Olsen is a leading specialist manufacturer of materials testing equipment to measure material’s strength and performance.

Elastocon manufactures instruments for testing and analysis of rubber and plastic materials.

Aboni specialize in the manufacture of instruments determining the residual water content in solids.
